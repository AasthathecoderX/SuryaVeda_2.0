# SuryaVeda — Pitch Deck 



> "SuryaVeda is an AI-powered platform that tells Indian households exactly how much electricity they consume, how much solar energy their rooftop can generate, and whether going solar is worth the investment — all in under 30 seconds."

---

## The Problem We Solve

1. **Indian households overpay on electricity** — most people don't know their actual consumption breakdown or which appliances cost them the most.
2. **Solar adoption is slow despite great potential** — India gets 300+ sunny days/year, but homeowners don't know if solar "makes sense" for *their* specific roof, location, and budget.
3. **Existing solar calculators are generic** — they don't combine your actual electricity usage with local solar potential to give personalized, actionable answers.

**In short:** People want to go solar but don't have the data to decide confidently.

---

## What SuryaVeda Does (Demo Flow)

### Step 1: Know Your Consumption
- Enter your appliances (fans, AC, fridge, etc.) and usage hours
- AI predicts your monthly bill using an XGBoost model trained on 45,000+ Indian households
- *"You're using ~234 kWh/month, costing ₹1,757"*

### Step 2: Discover Solar Potential
- Enter your location (or auto-detect via GPS)
- AI predicts how much energy solar panels would generate on YOUR rooftop
- *"A 5kW system at your location would generate 7,343 kWh/year"*

### Step 3: See the Full Picture
- Insights page shows: annual savings, payback period, carbon offset
- *"You'd save ₹55,000/year, pay back the system in 5.5 years, and offset 6 tonnes of CO₂"*

### Step 4: Model Long-Term Returns
- ROI Calculator factors in tariff inflation, panel degradation, maintenance
- Year-by-year breakdown showing when you break even and total profit over 25 years

## Technical Highlights (For Technical Audiences)

- **XGBoost Regressor** for electricity (45,345 records, R² = 0.9999 on the checked-in dataset)
- **Gradient Boosting Regressor** for solar irradiance prediction (387 records, R² = 0.948 on the checked-in dataset)
- **Smart fallback system**: ML model for typical usage (95–926 kWh), formula-based for edge cases
- **Seasonal adjustments**: Summer months automatically boost AC/fan consumption estimates
- **Real-time weather data**: Open-Meteo API for cloud coverage auto-detection
- **JWT authentication** with bcrypt password hashing
- **Full-stack**: React 19 frontend + Flask backend + SQLite

## Future Improvements (Roadmap)

### Game Changers (Major)

1. **Upload Your Electricity Bill (OCR)**
   - User takes a photo of their bill → app extracts consumption data automatically
   - No manual input needed, more accurate baseline
   - Tech: Azure Document Intelligence or Tesseract OCR

2. **Real-Time Solar Monitoring Dashboard**
   - Connect to actual solar inverter APIs (Enphase, Growatt, etc.)
   - Show live generation vs predicted, alert on underperformance
   - Compare actual ROI vs projected

3. **Government Subsidy Calculator**
   - India has PM Surya Ghar (₹78,000 subsidy for <3kW systems)
   - Auto-calculate effective cost after subsidy based on state + system size
   - Would significantly improve payback period numbers

4. **Community Comparison**
   - "Homes like yours in Bangalore typically use X kWh"
   - Anonymized benchmarking to motivate conservation

5. **Multi-Language Support**
   - Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi
   - Solar adoption is highest in Tier-2/3 cities where English isn't primary

### Minor Improvements (Polish)

1. Auto-fill ROI Calculator from solar prediction results
2. Export predictions as PDF report (shareable with family/installer)
3. Add more appliances (geyser, washing machine, EV charger)
4. Electricity slab-based billing (Indian states have tiered rates)
5. Push notifications for "best months to install solar"
6. Comparison mode: side-by-side different system sizes
7. Mobile-responsive improvements for smaller screens
